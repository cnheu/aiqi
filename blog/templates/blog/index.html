One in Five Australians suffer from Asthma and Allergies

Allergic diseases like asthma, excema and hayfever are incurable, chronic diseases.

There are over 100,000 hospital admissions each year due to these illnesses.

They prey on the most vulnerable in our society: young children and the elderly.

Prevention is Better than Cure

The worst can be prevented if we:

1. Know the likelihood of an attack happening

2. Understand the triggers that affect us

3. Respond with an effective action plan
